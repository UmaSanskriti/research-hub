## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my research data private?
Yes. Your research library is stored locally and is not shared with anyone unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

### Do I need to pay for API access to academic databases?
No. We use free, publicly accessible APIs from Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex, and Crossref.

### Can I export my data?
Yes. You can export your paper collection, researcher profiles, and notes in standard formats (CSV, JSON, BibTeX).

### How often is the data updated?
Citation counts and researcher profiles are refreshed whenever you view them, ensuring you always have current information.

### What if a paper isn't in any database?
You can manually add papers with basic metadata. The system will still help you organize and connect them to your other research.

### Can multiple people use the same Research Hub?
Currently, Research Hub is designed for individual use. Team features are on our roadmap.
